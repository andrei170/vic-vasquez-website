About Vic

Eighteen years of Central Valley real estate, in one operator.

Vic Vazquez is the CEO and Acquisitions lead at Sunlight Capital Inc., the Bakersfield-based investment firm that wholesales and resells residential properties across four counties. He is also the Designated Broker at Agentcor Realty, a Central Valley brokerage with seventy-plus licensed agents.

He has been a licensed California broker since 2018 and an active operator in Bakersfield real estate for eighteen years. The combination gives the buyer list one thing it usually does not get from either a wholesaler or a brokerage alone: a single operator with both the deal pipeline and the licensed-broker accountability on the same side of the table.

Off the deals, Vic hosts The Vic Vazquez Podcast and posts daily on Instagram and TikTok at @realvic_vasquez, where the audience runs from first-time investors to operators with full portfolios.
