01

After-Repair Value (ARV)

What your property would sell for fully renovated, based on recent comparable sales within a quarter-mile radius. We pull the comps live on the call so you can see them.

02

Estimated Repair Cost

Walk-through estimate from a contractor's eye. Roof, HVAC, foundation, plumbing, electrical, cosmetics. We err on the conservative side because surprise costs eat margin.

03

Holding & Transaction

Insurance, taxes, utilities while we own it; closing costs both ways; agent commissions on resale; financing if applicable. The boring stuff that's real money.

04

Resale Margin

The profit we need to make this worthwhile. We're transparent about it. If our margin makes our offer too low for your situation, listing is the right move and we'll tell you so.
